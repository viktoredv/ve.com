Terms of Use
Terms of Use is a design experiment that questions under what terms we as users navigate in the world of data-opolies.
Terms of Use is a speculative re-design object that exists in a near-possible future, where data-opolies are no longer independent, but are controlled fully or partially by governments. In this case, the american president, through executive order, has extorted the company to customize under what terms users of Happy Times, a fictive but recognizable service, use the service. Through a small, but considerable change in this juridical document, that almost no-one reads, the authorities are given access to all data; likes, content, messages, without having to adhere to any formal juridical process in the transfer. The terms also hint of a new feature which will enable and encourage the possibility to report on propaganda content. These reports, and this data can then be used for police and military intervention. If you act in a certain way; upload "propaganda", has the wrong opinions, like the wrong content, or write with the wrong people, don’t be surprised if the military comes knocking on your door.
